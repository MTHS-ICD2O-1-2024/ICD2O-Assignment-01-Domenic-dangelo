Chai Keong Toh

Early Life

Chai Keong Toh was born 1965 in Singapore. He studied in Singapore Polytechnic. He received his university education from the university of Cambridge in the United Kingdom.

MANETs

Chai Keong Toh is considered a pionner in the area of mobile ad hoc networks. His work laid foundation for modern wireless communcation systems in areas such as emergency response and military communications. His work was helpful in developing a more efficient communication systems in modern times.

Smart Cities and IoT

Chai keong Toh played a major role in the advancement of smart cities, particularly in the development of IoT (Internet of Things) technologies. His work on the systems helped with enabling more efficient urban infrastructure management. His work also looks into how they can optimize services such as traffic management, public safety, energy efficiency, and healthcare. His research has provided city planners with a way to make a city that can ensuring long-term sustainability and efficient data management.

Awards

  • IEEE Kiyo Tomiyasu Award in 2005
  • IET Ambrose Fleming Medal in 2009
  • He was also elected as a Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering in 2019
